Privacy Policy for WhatsApp Tools Extension

Last Updated: March 6, 2025

1. Information Collection

This extension collects and stores message history locally on your device using IndexedDB. No data is transmitted to external servers.

2. Data Usage

The phone numbers and messages you send are stored locally to:

  • Track which numbers have been contacted
  • Allow filtering of previously contacted numbers
  • Enable export of message history

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser and is not shared with any third parties.

4. Permissions

This extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To interact with WhatsApp Web
  • scripting: To execute scripts on WhatsApp Web
  • clipboardWrite: To copy extracted phone numbers
  • storage: To store message history
  • tabs: To manage WhatsApp Web tabs

5. WhatsApp Terms Compliance

Users are responsible for ensuring their use of this extension complies with WhatsApp's Terms of Service. Mass messaging may violate WhatsApp's anti-spam policies and could result in account restrictions.
